About Smith County Soccer Academy

Founded by Erling Johnsen, Smith County Soccer Academy is dedicated to nurturing young soccer talent at Rose City Soccer Complex in Tyler, Texas.

We provide holistic player development through professional coaching in a supportive, fun environment. Our programs focus on technical skills, tactical understanding, physical fitness, and character building — preparing players for success on and off the field.

Whether your child is taking their first touch or aiming for competitive play, we're here to help them grow with passion and confidence.

Meet Our Coaches

Erling Johnsen

Founder & Head Coach

With years of experience in youth development and a deep passion for the game, Erling leads the academy with a focus on skill, character, and enjoyment. USSF licensed and committed to creating a positive impact in the community.
