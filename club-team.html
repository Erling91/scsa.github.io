Our Competitive Club Team Program

The Club Team is the elite pathway at Smith County Soccer Academy – designed for committed players ready to take their game to the next level. Building on our Youth Academy foundation, this program focuses on high-level training, league play, tournaments, and player development toward collegiate and professional opportunities.

Key Features

  • Year-round professional coaching and tactical development
  • Competitive league participation and showcase tournaments
  • Individual player evaluations and goal-setting
  • Strength, conditioning, and mental performance training
  • Pathway scouting exposure and college recruitment support
