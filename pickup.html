Pick-Up Soccer

Join us for exciting adult pick-up soccer games at Rose City Soccer Complex every Friday from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. It's a great opportunity to enjoy the beautiful game, connect with fellow soccer enthusiasts, and stay active.


Details:


  • Date: Every Friday
  • Time: 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Location: Rose City Soccer Complex
  • Cost: $5 to join (Cash/Venmo/Zelle)

Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking to have some fun on the field, everyone is welcome. Lace up your boots, bring your A-game, and let's make Friday evenings a soccer-filled experience!

For any additional information or inquiries, feel free to contact us. We look forward to seeing you on the pitch!
