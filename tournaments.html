Rise of the Golden Knight 2026 Small-Sided Tournament Series

In 2026 we launch Rise of the Golden Knight – six epic tournaments that tell one continuous medieval adventure played out on the pitch. Athletes from Smith County and beyond will fight through raw 1v1 duels all the way to the final 7v7 siege.

One player who shows the most skill, heart, and leadership all year will be crowned the Golden Knight.
